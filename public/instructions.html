MOVE AND FIRE

Use and arrow keys to move spaceship in left and right direction.
Press F key to fire lasers to destroy meteors. If the particular key for firing lasers is pressed for a log period of time then lasers will fire automatically.

DAMAGE,SCORE & HEALTH

If lasers hits meteor it gives +50 points.If meteor hits spaceship then health reduces and same case with if meteor passes down it with increase the damage (%). The and of heath and damage based on level.

ALERT SOUNDS

At three conditions during gameplay sounds will be invoked. First, when lasers hit meteors. For, second and third cases alert sound will play when spaceship health is <=20 and damage (%) >=75

HIGHSCORES

For the Highscore to saved on the leadboards page, any player must score above 40,000. Otherwise at the end of the game the save functionality of the game will be disabled and they have to try until a reasonable score is reached.

LEVELS & DIFFICULTY

There are three difficulty levels Normal, Hard, Insane. As can be understood by name subsequent levels, it will be more tougher than the previous ones i.e. meteors will fall at greater number at faster rates.

OTHER THINGS

FPS shows the current fps of the game. Remember not to allow any meteor to pass through the influence of spaceship of health of spaceship to reduced to zero otherwise it will be game over. Also, at top-right corner pause/play button is tere If you want to take a break.
